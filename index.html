web-polls

An easy and pleasant way to create and participate in polls.

Try our app
You need to create polls for your courses? Need to know what people think of your association / application? Need feedback on something? We have you covered! With web-polls, you can easily create your own polls, invite the people you need and ask them just about anything.

A simple way to manage rooms and polls.

Easily check which poll is active and add rooms and polls as you like. Even when a poll is finished, the results can still be seen by clicking on the corresponding poll in the list.

A simple interface to create questions.

A simple and straightforward interface is used to quickly create and edit questions, be it either single or multiple-answers questions.

Answer and see the results evolving.

After answering to a question, you can see the results on a nice graph, updated in real time.
